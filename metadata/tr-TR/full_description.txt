<b>Food You</b> is a powerful, open-source nutrition tracker built with a modern Material You aesthetic. It helps you track not just calories, but your entire nutrient intake — with reliable food data and a flexible, modular interface.

<b>Özellikler:</b>
<ul>
    <li><b>Material You Design:</b> Dynamic theming that adapts to your device for a seamless visual experience.</li>
    <li><b>Comprehensive Food Databases:</b> Access nutrition data from Open Food Facts, USDA FoodCentral, and the Swiss Food Composition Database.</li>
    <li><b>Full Nutrition Tracking:</b> Monitor calories, macros, vitamins, minerals, and more.</li>
    <li><b>Recipe Creation:</b> Combine foods into custom recipes with automatic nutrition calculation.</li>
    <li><b>Daily Goals:</b> Set and follow personalized nutrition targets.</li>
    <li><b>Modular Home Screen:</b> Customize your home with cards tailored to your needs.</li>
</ul>

Start tracking your nutrition today with Food You — clean, complete, and fully yours.
